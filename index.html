Project I

This dashboard analyzes logistics operations with a focus on operational efficiency, delivery performance, fuel cost management, and route productivity. The analysis was carried out using trip, delivery, driver, route, and fuel purchase data to better understand how different aspects of the logistics process impact overall business performance.

The dashboard highlights key operational metrics such as total trips completed, average trip hours, fuel cost per trip, and on-time delivery rate. It also provides insights into driver productivity, route efficiency, yearly trip trends, and fuel expenses across truck manufacturers. The aim of the analysis is to identify operational strengths, uncover inefficiencies, and provide recommendations that can support better decision-making and improved logistics performance.

Project II

This Sales Performance Overview dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the company’s sales activities, profitability, customer behavior, and cost distribution across different regions and product categories. The dashboard highlights key business metrics such as total revenue of ₦2.33 billion, total profit of ₦465.67 million, cost of goods sold (COGS) amounting to ₦1.86 billion, and a customer base of 2,098. Through interactive visualizations, stakeholders can easily monitor sales representative performance, identify the most profitable products, evaluate regional cost distribution, and track customer trends over time. The dashboard is structured to support quick decision-making by presenting financial and operational insights in a simple and visually appealing format.

In addition, the dashboard enables management to compare performance across product categories such as Electronics, Furniture, and Home Appliances, as well as across regions including East, North, South, and West. The sales representative analysis helps identify top-performing staff members, while the product profitability section reveals which products contribute the most to overall profit. The customer trend visualization also provides insight into customer engagement patterns over several months, helping management detect unusual fluctuations that may require urgent attention. Overall, the dashboard serves as an effective business intelligence tool for tracking organizational performance and supporting strategic planning.

Project III

This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of Superstore’s sales performance, profit trends, customer distribution, and regional contribution. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly understand overall business performance by presenting key metrics in a clear and visual format. Through interactive insights, stakeholders can easily identify patterns in revenue generation, track profitability over time, and assess how different factors such as product categories, customer segments, and sales regions contribute to total performance.

In addition, the dashboard helps uncover critical areas of concern, particularly profitability challenges linked to certain segments and discount levels. It enables decision-makers to pinpoint underperforming regions, evaluate the impact of pricing strategies, and understand customer behaviour across different markets. By combining these insights, the dashboard supports more informed strategic planning, helping the business optimize operations, improve profit margins, and make data-driven decisions that enhance overall efficiency and growth.
